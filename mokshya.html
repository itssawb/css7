    •         Now Let us know about Dhapasi Rath/Sindur/Biska/bisket Jatra, Generally Known as Dhapasi Rath Jatra. It was first started in Dhapasi from 2064 B.S. According to our Old Generation People, They used to go TOKHA to celebrate Jatra along with Cock to Sacrifice. Dhapasi Rath jatra is celebrated 3 days in a year, Baisakh 1 to 3.
